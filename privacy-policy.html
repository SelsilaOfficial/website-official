privacy & policy

Effective Date: March 24, 2025
Selsila World Limited (“Selsila”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal data you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website [https://selsiworld.com], use our mobile applications, browser extensions, or any other services we provide (collectively, the “Services”).
By accessing or using our Services, you agree to this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Use. If you do not agree, please do not use our Services.
1. Our Commitment to Your Privacy
- Privacy First: Selsila is built with user privacy at the core of its mission.
- No Required Identity Info: You are not required to provide your name, email, or phone number to access most parts of our Services.
- No Marketing via IP: We never use your IP address for targeted advertising marketing.
- Optional Analytics: You can opt out of certain tracking technologies and analytics.
- Transparent Practices: This Policy maybe updated as our privacy practices evolve or as legal requirements change.
2. Information We Collect
a. Information You Provide Voluntarily
We may collect the following information when voluntarily submitted by you:
- Your username, avatar, and any media you upload (e.g., for minting collectibles).
- Your email address and any information you provide through customer support, surveys, promotions, or newsletter subscriptions.
b. Information Collected Automatically
We automatically collect certain information when you use our Services, including:
- Device and browser information (type, OS, model, identifiers).
- Access details (times, pages visited, links clicked, referring URLs).
- IP address (used only for system security, localization, and compliance—not for profiling or advertising).
- Public blockchain data (e.g., wallet addresses) required to deliver blockchain-based Services.
c. Information from Third Parties
If you sign in using a third-party platform (e.g., Google), we may receive limited profile information per that platform’s settings.
3. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect for the following lawful purposes:
- To Provide Services: To deliver, operate, and support our Services.
- To Personalize Experience: To customize content and improve your user experience.
- To Improve Functionality: To analyze usage trends and enhance Service performance.
- To Communicate: To send you updates, offers, and other information you may be interested in. You can opt out at any time.
- To Comply with Law: To meet legal obligations, including anti-fraud and security requirements.
4. How We Share Your Information
We may share your information in the following cases:
- With Consent: At your direction or with your consent.
- With Affiliates: Within our corporate family under strict confidentiality terms.
- With Service Providers: Vendors supporting our operations (e.g., hosting, analytics, security).
- Legal Requirements: If required by law or government requests.
- Business Transfers: If we undergo a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.
- We do not sell or rent your personal information. We also do not share your personal data for third-party marketing or targeted advertising.
5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies
We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience. Cookies help us:
- Understand usage behavior
- Facilitate login and session management
- Monitor analytics for performance and stability
You can control cookies through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect site functionality.
Types of Cookies Used:
- Essential Cookies: Required for Service functionality
- Performance Cookies: Help us analyze and improve
- Preference Cookies: Store yourpreferences
- Analytics Cookies: Measure user interaction
We currently do not respond to “Do Not Track” browser settings.
6. Your Rights & Choices (U.S. Residents)
Depending on your state of residence (e.g., California, Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut), you may have the right to:
- Access, correct, or delete your personal data
- Know what information we collect and how we use it
- Opt out of personal data processing (we don’t sell/share)
- Appeal a denied privacy request
- Use an authorized agent to act on your behalf
To exercise your rights, email us at: support@selsiworld.com We will verify your identity before proceeding. We do not discriminate against individuals who exercise their privacy rights.
7. Children’s Privacy
Our Services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without verifiable parental consent. If we learn we have collected such data, we will delete it promptly.
8. Data Retention
We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or to meet legal, accounting, or reporting obligations.
9. Data Security
We implement technical and organizational safeguards to secure your data, including encryption, access controls, and secure server infrastructure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
10. International Users
Selsila World Limited operates globally and provides Services accessible across jurisdictions, including but not limited to the United States, the European Union (EU), the United Kingdom (UK), Canada, and Southeast Asia. We are not headquartered in the United States; however, we take privacy and data protection seriously, regardless of your location. Cross-Border Data Transfers
When you use our Services, your personal information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence, including jurisdictions that may have different data protection standards. These transfers are essential to operate the Services and provide you with global functionality.
Your Consent and Rights By accessing or using our Services, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in countries outside of your own, including to those where data protection laws may be less stringent. Regardless of the transfer location, we uphold strong privacy and security principles to protect your personal information.
11. Changes to This Policy
This Policy may be revised from time to time. When updated, we will revise the “Effective Date” and notify you through appropriate means (e.g., website banner or email notice). We recommend reviewing this Policy regularly.
